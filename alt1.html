About

Why I built this

I'm an independent developer and systems thinker who spends most of my time building automated tools — trading bots, content pipelines, mobile apps. I apply the same logic to everything: find the inefficiency, build the system, remove the friction.

Personal finance was broken for me in the same way it's broken for most people. Not because the math is hard — but because no one hands you the right questions.

If you can ask an AI to write your emails, you can ask it to fix your finances.

Prompted Finance is that. One prompt a week, tested on real numbers, designed to take less time than your morning coffee. No courses to buy, no spreadsheets to maintain, no finance degree required.
